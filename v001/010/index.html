UX DESIGNER | NYC

My name is Maki Hirose ( /mah-kee hy-roaz/ ) and I am a UX Designer with Full Stack Web Development skills and over ten years of visual experience in the Commercial Photography Industry. I am empathic towards Users and I am an advocate of Web Developers. I strive to create app ideas that are functional for the User and reasonably executable by the Coding Team.

In my past role as an Advertising Photographer, I was an expert at color, composition and paying close attention to detail. Photoshoots required me to be prepared, organized and articulate. My leadership, project management and client-facing skills were essential when working with Advertising Clients like L’Oréal, Maybelline and Smirnoff. I shot images for both web apps and print, working alongside Directors of Ideation and Brand Managers. These are values I bring to a UX position.

I studied UX Design at General Assembly, Full Stack Web Development at Columbia University and Photography & Imaging at New York University. I am bilingual in English and Japanese.

I have experience with web technologies including React, CSS, Node, Express and MongoDB. I also have experience with visual software including InVision, Sketch, XD, Photoshop and Final Cut Pro.

I have spent half of my life in London, and the other half in New York City.
